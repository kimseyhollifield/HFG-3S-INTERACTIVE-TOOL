Direct equity ownership

Variable Annuities
Market-linked with optional riders
Managed Accounts
Professionally allocated portfolios
REITs & Alternatives
Real estate, commodities, etc.
Roof Characteristics
↑ Fluctuates with the market — up and down

⚠ Subject to market loss — not protected
✓ Only money here you can afford to stay invested through a downturn
